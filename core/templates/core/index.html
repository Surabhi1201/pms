Project

A project is a temporary endeavor undertaken to create a unique product, service, or result. It is characterized by a defined beginning and end in terms of time, as well as specific objectives to be achieved within that timeframe. Projects are typically undertaken to bring about beneficial change or to add value to an organization, business, community, or individual.

Task

A task is a specific and discrete piece of work that needs to be accomplished within a project or as part of ongoing operations. Tasks are smaller components of a project or larger goal, and they contribute to the overall achievement of objectives
